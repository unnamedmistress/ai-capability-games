# AI Capability Atlas - Learning Content

## Introduction

AI capability literacy is essential for understanding how AI systems function, their limitations, and their potential. It empowers individuals to leverage AI effectively, ensuring ethical and informed usage across various domains.

## The 3 Core Domains

### 1. Knowledge & Learning (Understanding Mode)
Focuses on AI's ability to acquire, process, and apply information.

**Key Skills:**
- Explain Complex Concepts
- Summarize Texts
- Research & Verification
- Fact-Checking

### 2. Creativity & Content (Generative Mode)
Covers AI's role in generating novel ideas, art, and content.

**Key Skills:**
- Brainstorm Ideas
- Draft Content
- Transform Content
- Edit & Refine

### 3. Analysis & Problem Solving (Analytical Mode)
Involves AI's capacity to analyze complex problems and optimize solutions.

**Key Skills:**
- Compare Options
- Evaluate Risk
- Decompose Problems
- Build Workflows
